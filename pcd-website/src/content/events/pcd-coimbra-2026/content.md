PCD @ Coimbra (Portugal) had its 4th edition this year.

Our event took place in March and we now have an ongoing exhibition with the works that were accepted to the edition open call.

This year, the event took place March 4 and March 11. It had two keynotes (Lena Weber, Lisa Moura), Short presentations, 1 workshop (Designing Generative Kinetic Cards) and two exhibitions.
